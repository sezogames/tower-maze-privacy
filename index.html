Tower Maze

Privacy Policy

This page explains what limited data Tower Maze may use, why it is used, and how player data may be stored for gameplay, purchases, rewards, and leaderboard or cloud features.

Game: Tower Maze
Publisher: SeZo Games
Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. Data we may collect

  • Progress, settings, lives, skins, and economy data stored on your device.
  • Technical transaction data needed for rewarded ads and in-app purchases.
  • Scores, player labels, and sync data when leaderboard or cloud save features are enabled.
  • Referral data: a referral code linked to your account and a record of which player invited you, when you use the friend-invite feature.
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information used to detect, fix, and prevent problems and keep the game stable.

2. Why this data is used

  • To save and restore your game.
  • To process rewarded ads and purchases.
  • To provide leaderboard, cloud save, and account sync features.
  • To grant friend-invite (referral) rewards to both the inviter and the invited player.
  • To support security, debugging, and service reliability.

3. Third-party services

The game may use third-party services for ads, purchases, platform features, and cloud or leaderboard functions. These include Google AdMob (advertising and advertising identifiers) and Google Firebase (analytics, crash diagnostics, anonymous authentication, and cloud storage used for cloud save, leaderboard, and referral features). Those services handle data under their own privacy policies.

4. Data storage and deletion

Tower Maze (by SeZo Games) stores progress and settings on your device and, when cloud features are enabled, a limited set of data in our cloud services (Google Firebase). You can request deletion of your data as follows:

  • On-device data: uninstall the app, or clear the app's data from your device Settings. This immediately removes the progress, settings, and economy data stored on the device.
  • Cloud / account data: email oguzhansezgin.ie@gmail.com with the subject "Data deletion" and your in-game name (and referral code, if you have one) so we can locate and delete your record.

Data deleted on request: your player name / nickname, anonymous user ID, saved scores and leaderboard entry, and referral codes and referral records.

Retention: deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Purchase and transaction records may be retained where required for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes. Anonymous analytics and crash data are kept only for the limited periods set by the respective Google services.

5. Children and parents

Tower Maze is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Advertising shown in the game is not personalized for users identified as under 13. Use of the game should follow the rules that apply in your region, and parent or guardian supervision is recommended for children.

6. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version shown in the game or on this page is the active version.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at oguzhansezgin.ie@gmail.com, or use the support contact listed on the game's store page.
