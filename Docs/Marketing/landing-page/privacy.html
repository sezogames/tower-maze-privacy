Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

TowerMaze ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

TowerMaze collects minimal information necessary to provide and improve the game experience:

  • Device Identifiers: We collect device identifiers and advertising IDs for ad delivery and analytics purposes.
  • Gameplay Analytics: We track gameplay data such as session length, levels reached, score milestones, and game events. This helps us understand how players interact with the game and identify areas for improvement.
  • No Personal Information: We do NOT collect your name, email address, age, or other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect precise location data. We may use approximate location for advertising purposes only.

Third-Party Services

TowerMaze uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the game. AdMob may collect device identifiers and other information to deliver personalized ads. For more information about how Google handles your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Unity Analytics

We use Unity Analytics to collect gameplay data and improve the game. Unity may process analytics data to help us understand player behavior and game performance. For more information, review Unity's Privacy Policy.

Advertising

By default, AdMob may display personalized ads based on your device's advertising ID and interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • On iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads and toggle off.
  • On Android: Go to Settings > Google > Manage your Google Account > Data & Privacy > Ad personalization and toggle off.

Even if you opt out, you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized.

Data Storage

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT sync your game progress to cloud servers or transfer your data to external servers. Your game saves remain entirely on your device unless you explicitly export them.

Children's Privacy

TowerMaze is NOT directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of TowerMaze after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
