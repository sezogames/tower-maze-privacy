## 1. Overview

Tower Maze ("the App") is developed and maintained by SeZo Games ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information directly.
- We do **not** require account registration.
- We do **not** access your camera, microphone, contacts, or files.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

The following data may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into the App:

| Data Type | Purpose | Collected By |
|-----------|---------|--------------|
| Device model & OS version | Crash reporting, performance analytics | Firebase / Crashlytics |
| Advertising ID (GAID) | Ad personalization, frequency capping | Google AdMob |
| App usage events (e.g., level start, game over) | Analytics and product improvement | Firebase Analytics |
| IP address (approximate location/region) | Ad serving | Google AdMob |
| Crash logs and stack traces | Bug fixing | Firebase Crashlytics |
| In-app purchase data | Transaction verification | Google Play Billing |

## 4. Advertising

The App displays ads served by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

- You can **opt out of personalized ads** at any time:
  - Android: **Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID** (Android 12+) or **Opt out of Ads Personalization**
- We comply with Google's EU User Consent Policy (UMP). If you are located in the EEA/UK, you will be shown a **consent dialog** before any personalized ads are served.

## 5. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases through **Google Play Billing**. We do not store or process any payment information ourselves. All transactions are handled securely by Google Play.
